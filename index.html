Ginkgo biloba Mariken

A little bit of History

Ginkgo is a species of tree native to China. It is widely used as a landscape plant because of the extremely appealing fan-shaped leaves. Biloba means 2 lobes which makes reference to the leaves shape. Ginkgos have been around for millions of years so they are considered a living fossil.

Ginkgo biloba Mariken is a Ginkgo variety known for staying quite small (around 3 feet max height). Most ginkgos are full sized trees so this variey is great for limited spaces and small gardens.

This cultivar originated as witch's broom found in 1995 by Piet Vergeldt in Kronenburger Park, Nijmegen, The Netherlands. (according to the American Conifer Society)
